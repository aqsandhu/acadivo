## Role Description

As a **Student**, you use Acadivo to stay organized and connected with your school. You can:

- View your class timetable
- See assigned homework and submit before deadlines
- View your marks and overall results
- Track your attendance record
- Ask teachers questions in the Q&A forum
- Chat with teachers and school admin
- Receive important notifications

> **Note:** Students can only view their own data. You cannot see other students' marks or attendance.

## Viewing Timetable

### Weekly Timetable

1. Go to **Timetable > Weekly**
2. View your complete weekly schedule
3. Each period shows:
   - Subject name
   - Teacher name
   - Room number
   - Time

### Today's Classes

1. The dashboard shows today's classes automatically
2. Click any class to see details
3. See which teacher is teaching and what subject

### Timetable Notifications

- If a timetable changes, you receive a notification
- Changes are highlighted in the timetable view

## Submitting Homework

### Viewing Assigned Homework

1. Go to **Homework > Assigned**
2. View all pending homework with:
   - Title and description
   - Subject
   - Due date (with countdown)
   - Status: Pending / Submitted / Late
3. Click any homework to see details

### Submitting Homework

1. Open the homework assignment
2. Read the instructions carefully
3. In the **Your Submission** section:
   - Type your answer in the text box
   - Or upload files (PDF, Word, images)
   - Maximum file size: 20 MB
4. Click **Submit**
5. You receive a confirmation notification

### After Submission

- Status changes to "Submitted"
- Teacher reviews and grades
- You receive a notification when graded
- View your marks and feedback in the homework details

### Late Submissions

- Submissions after the due date are marked "Late"
- Some teachers may deduct marks for late work
- Always try to submit before the deadline!

## Viewing Marks

### Subject-wise Marks

1. Go to **Marks > Subject-wise**
2. View marks for each subject:
   - Exam type (Quiz, Midterm, Final, etc.)
   - Total marks
   - Marks obtained
   - Percentage
   - Grade (A+, A, B, C, D, F)
3. Click any entry for detailed view

### Marks Trends

1. Go to **Marks > Trends**
2. See your performance over time
3. Graph shows marks trend for each subject
4. Identify subjects where you need improvement

## Viewing Results

### Term Results

1. Go to **Results > Term Results**
2. View results for each term:
   - First Term
   - Second Term
   - Third Term
   - Final
3. Each result shows:
   - Total marks
   - Obtained marks
   - Percentage
   - Grade
   - Rank in class (if published)
   - Teacher remarks
   - Principal remarks

### Result Card

1. Open any result
2. View subject-wise breakdown
3. Click **Download PDF** to save result card
4. Share with parents via WhatsApp or email

## Attendance Tracking

### Viewing Your Attendance

1. Go to **Attendance > My Record**
2. View attendance for the current month
3. Each day shows:
   - Date
   - Status (Present, Absent, Late, Leave, Half Day)
   - Subject/period (if period-wise)

### Attendance Summary

1. Go to **Attendance > Summary**
2. View:
   - Total days in month
   - Present count
   - Absent count
   - Late count
   - Overall percentage
3. Warning shown if below 75%

### Monthly Calendar View

1. Go to **Attendance > Calendar**
2. Color-coded calendar shows each day's status
3. Green = Present, Red = Absent, Yellow = Late

## Q&A Forum

### Asking a Question

1. Go to **Q&A > Ask**
2. Select subject category
3. Type your question clearly
4. Add attachments if helpful (diagrams, screenshots)
5. Click **Post Question**
6. Relevant teachers are notified

### Viewing Your Questions

1. Go to **Q&A > My Questions**
2. See all questions you've asked
3. View answers from teachers
4. Upvote helpful answers

### Searching Questions

1. Go to **Q&A > Browse**
2. Search by keyword
3. Filter by subject
4. See questions asked by other students (and their answers)

### Best Practices

- Search before asking — your question may already be answered
- Be specific in your question
- Use proper language (avoid slang)
- Don't share personal information
- Be respectful to teachers and other students

## Messaging

### Sending a Message

1. Go to **Messages > New**
2. Search for your teacher or school admin
3. Type your message
4. Click **Send**

### Receiving Messages

- New message notifications appear at the top
- Click the bell icon to preview
- Go to **Messages > Inbox** for full conversations

### When to Message

| Situation | Who to Message |
|-----------|---------------|
| Homework question | Subject teacher |
| Can't submit homework | Subject teacher |
| Mark seems incorrect | Subject teacher |
| Attendance error | Class teacher or Admin |
| General school question | School Admin |
| Emergency | School Admin or Principal |

## FAQ

**Q: I can't see my timetable. Why?**
A: Ask your School Admin to confirm you are assigned to a class and section. The timetable is created per class-section.

**Q: I submitted homework but it's still showing "Pending."**
A: Refresh the page. If still pending, check your internet connection. The mobile app will sync automatically when online.

**Q: Can I see other students' marks?**
A: No. For privacy, you can only view your own marks and results.

**Q: My attendance shows wrong. What should I do?**
A: Message your class teacher or School Admin with the specific date and correct status.

**Q: How do I download my result card?**
A: Go to Results > Term Results, open your result, and click Download PDF.

**Q: Can I use Acadivo on my phone?**
A: Yes! Download the Acadivo app from Play Store or App Store. All features work on mobile.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email and tenant code. A reset link will be sent.

## Tips and Tricks

1. **Check Homework Daily**: Visit the homework section every day after school to stay on top of assignments

2. **Set Reminders**: The app sends notifications, but you can also set personal reminders for due dates

3. **Offline Mode**: The mobile app works without internet. View timetable and homework offline

4. **Dark Mode**: Enable dark mode from settings for comfortable night study

5. **Urdu Support**: Switch the app to Urdu from settings if you prefer

6. **Quick Links**: Add the web app to your phone's home screen for one-tap access

7. **Parent Connection**: Make sure your parent is linked to your account so they can see your progress
