## Role Description

As a **Parent** or Guardian, Acadivo keeps you connected with your child's education. You can:

- View your child's progress (attendance, marks, homework, results)
- Pay school fees online via JazzCash or EasyPaisa
- Request detailed reports from teachers
- Ask teachers questions about your child's progress
- Chat with teachers and school administration
- Receive important school announcements and alerts

> **Note:** You can only see data for your linked children. You cannot view other students' information.

## Attendance Monitoring

### Viewing Attendance

1. Go to **Attendance > [Child Name]**
2. View monthly attendance record
3. See each day's status:
   - Present (green)
   - Absent (red)
   - Late (yellow)
   - Leave (blue)

### Attendance Summary

1. See overall percentage for the month
2. Warning shown if below 75%
3. Compare with previous months

### Low Attendance Alerts

- Automatic alerts when attendance drops below threshold
- You receive push notification and in-app alert
- Contact class teacher to discuss

## Homework Overview

### Viewing Assigned Homework

1. Go to **Homework > [Child Name]**
2. See all pending and completed homework
3. Each entry shows:
   - Title and subject
   - Due date
   - Status (Pending / Submitted / Graded)
   - Marks (if graded)

### Helping with Homework

1. Click any homework to see details
2. Read the teacher's instructions
3. See if your child has submitted
4. Download attachments (worksheets, reference material)

## Viewing Marks and Results

### Subject-wise Marks

1. Go to **Marks > [Child Name]**
2. View marks for each subject
3. See:
   - Exam type
   - Total and obtained marks
   - Percentage and grade

1. Go to **Results > [Child Name]**

## Fee Payment

### Viewing Fee Records

1. Go to **Fee > Records**
2. View all fee records for all children
3. Each record shows:
   - Fee type (Tuition, Admission, Exam, etc.)
   - Amount due
   - Due date
   - Status (Paid / Unpaid / Partial)
   - Balance

### Paying Fee Online

1. Find the unpaid fee record
2. Click **Pay Now**
3. Select payment method:
   - **JazzCash** (mobile wallet)
   - **EasyPaisa** (mobile wallet)
   - **Bank Transfer**
4. Enter payment details
5. Confirm payment
6. Receive confirmation notification
7. Download receipt as PDF

### Viewing Receipts

1. Go to **Fee > Receipts**
2. View all past payments
3. Click **Download** to save receipt PDF
4. Share receipts via WhatsApp or email

### Payment Methods Guide

| Method | How It Works | Processing Time |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| **JazzCash** | Enter JazzCash mobile number, confirm via app | Instant |
| **EasyPaisa** | Enter EasyPaisa mobile number, confirm via app | Instant |
| **Bank Transfer** | Transfer to school's bank account, upload proof | 1-2 business days |

## Requesting Reports

### Types of Reports

| Report | Contents | Best For |
|--------|----------|----------|
| **Progress Report** | Marks, attendance, overall performance | Parent-teacher meetings |
| **Attendance Report** | Detailed attendance with patterns | Understanding absences |
| **Behavior Report** | Conduct, discipline, participation | Addressing concerns |
| **Comprehensive Report** | All of the above | Complete overview |

### Requesting a Report

1. Go to **Reports > Request**
2. Select:
   - Child
   - Report type
   - Teacher (usually class teacher)
   - Remarks (reason for request)
3. Click **Request Report**
4. Teacher receives notification

### Viewing Completed Reports

1. Go to **Reports > Completed**
2. View ready reports
3. Download as PDF
4. Read teacher remarks carefully

## Q&A Forum

### Asking a Question

1. Go to **Q&A > Ask**
2. Select subject or general category
3. Type your question about your child's progress
4. Add details if needed
5. Click **Post Question**

### Example Questions

- "My child is struggling in Mathematics. What topics should we focus on?"
- "Can you explain why my child's attendance percentage dropped?"
- "When will the mid-term results be published?"
- "What extra activities can help my child's overall development?"

### Viewing Answers

1. Go to **Q&A > My Questions**
2. See all your questions and their answers
3. Teachers usually respond within 24-48 hours

## Messaging

### Sending a Message

1. Go to **Messages > New**
2. Search for your child's teacher or school admin
3. Type your message
4. Click **Send**

### Common Scenarios

| Scenario | Message To |
|----------|-----------|
| Child is sick (absence) | Class teacher |
| Fee payment issue | School Admin |
| Mark seems incorrect | Subject teacher |
| Want to meet teacher | Class teacher |
| General school query | School Admin |
| Emergency | School Admin or Principal |

### Receiving Messages

- New messages appear as notifications
- Click the bell icon to preview
- Go to **Messages > Inbox** for full view

## FAQ

**Q: I can only see one child, but I have two children in the school.**
A: Contact the School Admin and ask them to link your second child to your parent account.

**Q: My payment failed but money was deducted.**
A: Don't worry. Failed payments are usually auto-refunded within 24 hours. If not, contact School Admin with your transaction ID.

**Q: Can I pay fees for the whole year at once?**
A: Yes, if the school has created yearly fee structures. Otherwise, you pay monthly as records are generated.

**Q: How do I know if my child's attendance is too low?**
A: You receive an automatic alert when attendance drops below 75%. You can also check the attendance section anytime.

**Q: I requested a report but haven't received it.**
A: Reports usually take 2-3 days. If it's been longer, send a polite reminder message to the teacher.

**Q: Can I see my child's classmates' marks?**
A: No. For privacy, you can only see your own child's data.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Yes. Acadivo uses secure payment gateways. We do not store your card or wallet PIN.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email and school code. A reset link will be sent.

## Tips and Tricks

1. **Enable Notifications**: Allow push notifications so you never miss important alerts about attendance, homework, or fee dues

2. **Weekly Check**: Spend 5 minutes every weekend reviewing your child's homework and marks for the week

3. **Pay Early**: Pay fees within the first 10 days of the month to avoid late fees

4. **Keep Receipts**: Download and save all fee receipts for your records

5. **Communicate Regularly**: Don't wait for problems. Message teachers regularly to stay informed

6. **Urdu Support**: Switch the app to Urdu from settings if you prefer

7. **Add to Home Screen**: Add the Acadivo web app to your phone's home screen for quick access

8. **Multiple Children**: If you have multiple children, use the child selector at the top to switch between profiles
